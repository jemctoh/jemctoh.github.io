About

ふじの味

"Flavours of Fuji"


At ふじの味, we invite you on a delectable journey through the heart of Japan's rich culinary heritage.

Nestled in the heart of Singapore, our restaurant is a fusion of tradition and innovation, where every dish is a masterpiece crafted with precision and passion.

Master Chef Kenjiro Yamato

Chef Kenjiro Yamato

is a graduate of L'Ecole Tsuji Tokyo
(Tsuji Culinary Institute)

Since the tender age of 14, he apprenticed under his influential father, Master Chef Tokio Yamato, who heads one of Japan's premier sushi restaurants, Hashida Sushi in Tokyo.
